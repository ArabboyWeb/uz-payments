# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making `uz-payments` a professional, respectful, and safe
open-source project for developers building payment integrations.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence and clear reasoning.
- Do not share provider secrets, raw card data, customer data, or private merchant
  account information.
- Keep provider claims factual and based on official documentation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing secrets, raw card data, customer data, or private credentials.
- Misrepresenting this project as officially affiliated with a provider.
- Adding provider behavior based on guesses while presenting it as verified.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result
in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
